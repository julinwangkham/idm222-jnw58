Ghost Ship

Tools

Maya, Substance Painter, Unreal Engine 5

Overview

This project is meant to depict a captain's quarters during a terrible storm. The captain of the ship died trying to save it and now his spirit haunts the sea and whenever lighting strikes, the captain's ghostly figure appears. I modeled and surfaced the chandelier, bird cage, compass, trinket box, and barnacles with Maya and Substance Painter. The assembly of the scene and rain sim were done in Unreal Engine 5, where I also added layered materials of moss and grunge to the chandelier.
